About Us

Chopnaija is a food-specific e-commerce store set with the specific objective of exporting only the best made-in-Nigeria food items to the rest of the word. Our tagline, African food, made-in-Nigeria, made for the world sums up our essence. We understand that Nigerians are very widely travelled people and can be found in almost every part of the world. We seek to fulfil the cravings of home food. We provide a platform to shop quality and hygienically packaged local food items from indigenous local food processors to the diaspora market and thus help propel our local products to a global market. From ogbono, to egusi to zobo, smoked fish, kpomo or ijebu garri, or a selection of our indigenous local spices will give a sense of home feeling regardless of wherever you may be shopping from.
